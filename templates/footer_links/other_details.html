Returns & Exchange Policy

Important: You have 5 days from the delivery date to only exchange your items, return not allowed.

Return not allowed.

If you need to exchange an item, you can do so within 5 days of receiving your order. The item must be in its original condition and packaging to qualify for an exchange.

Exchange Process:

  • Login to your account and go to 'Orders'.
  • Select the item you want to exchange and choose the new item/size.
  • Submit the exchange request and follow the return instructions for the original item.

  • Can I return a used item? No, all returns must be unused and in their original condition.
  • What happens if my item is damaged? Please contact our support team immediately to initiate a return for damaged items.
  • How long does it take to process a return? Returns are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your item.
